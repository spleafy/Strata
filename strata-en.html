Strata, 20 years later

It is our 20th birthday and we will celebrate not only us, but all the people responsible for all that buzz and fuzz around in the last two decades here in Bulgaria. Masks off - marketing personalised.

It is a podcast to present marketing heroes - clients, agencies, production houses, media, print... you name it. We will talk friendly with friends - personality first. We will talk briefly - 20 questions top. We will present the person behind the business. We will discuss the global change, the rumors and the weather... and sometimes a little about marketing we will discuss.
